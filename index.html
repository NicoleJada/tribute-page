Wangari Maathai

First African woman to win the Nobel Peace Prize

Key Facts About Her Life:

  • 1940 - Born in Nyeri, Kenya
  • 1966 - Got Master’s degree in Biology from University of Pittsburgh
  • 1977 - Founded the Green Belt Movement to plant trees
  • 2002 - Elected to Kenyan Parliament
  • 2004 - Won Nobel Peace Prize for environmental work
  • 2011 - Passed away, but her movement lives on
